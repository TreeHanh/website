FAIRFARE - TRAVEL APP

FINAL PROTOTYPE

APP WEBSITE


Overview

“FairFare” is an app I designed for my interactive design class to help inform and notify travelers of flights to destinations they choose when the price falls within their price range.
PROBLEM: It's a hassle to constantly have to search flights to see if a price is within your price range or missing a good deal on a flight.
SOLUTION: - Provide an easy way for people to be notified on the price of a flight that they would be willing to purchase. Also help give people suggestion on when to book a flight by giving airfare predictions.
COMPETITIVE APPS: Kayak, Airfare Watchdog, Hipmunk, Google Flights



Takeaway


What I learned throughout this project is that the simpler it is the better it is. I noticed throughout each iteration of user testing that users prefer a clean UI that has less ambiguous buttons and features.


Ideation


Scenarios: I created several personas who were a part of my target audiences for the app. By doing so, I design my app around their problems and needs.


Wireframes and User Testing

Flows: Using Axure, I created a wireframe flow on how Veronica would use the app to find flights and also share with her friends.

Flows: This Larry's flow on how he would use the app to find flights to Seattle.


USABILITY TESTING: Using Flinto, I created a working prototype of the wireframes and recorded people using the app. From there I asked them questions on what to improve on the app and made changes.
